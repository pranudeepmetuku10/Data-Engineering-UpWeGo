# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for participant behavior and describes the process for addressing unacceptable behavior.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Demonstrating patience when helping others learn

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or threatening behavior
- Insulting comments or personal attacks
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Publishing private information without permission
- Trolling or spam
- Conduct that would be considered unprofessional in the workplace

## Enforcement

Community leaders will:

- Review reported incidents fairly and promptly
- Maintain confidentiality of reporters
- Take appropriate action to address unacceptable behavior
- Apply proportionate consequences

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue or reaching out directly to the project maintainers.
